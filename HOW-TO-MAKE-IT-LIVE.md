## â“ Troubleshooting

### Problem: "Site not found" or 404 error
**Solution:** 
- Wait 3-5 minutes (first deployment takes time)
- Clear browser cache (Ctrl + Shift + R)
- Make sure you merged the PR to main branch

### Problem: "GitHub Actions not showing"
**Solution:**
- Make sure you selected "GitHub Actions" in Pages settings
- Not "Deploy from a branch"

### Problem: "Workflow failed" (red X)
**Solution:**
- Click on the failed workflow to see details
- Usually resolves by re-running the workflow
- Click "Re-run all jobs"
